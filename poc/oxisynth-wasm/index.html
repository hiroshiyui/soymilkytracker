PoC: oxisynth SF2 synthesis in WASM

Proof-of-concept spike for SoymilkyTracker.
Fetches TimGM6mb.sf2, loads it with oxisynth running in WASM, renders A4 (Acoustic Grand Piano) to a PCM buffer, and plays it via AudioBufferSourceNode.

Status: idle — click to start

SF2: TimGM6mb (~5.7 MB, Tim Brechbill / M. Gorbe & E. Singer, GPL).
In the full app, the Freepats soundfont will be served and streamed the same way, driven by the tracker engine in an AudioWorklet.
